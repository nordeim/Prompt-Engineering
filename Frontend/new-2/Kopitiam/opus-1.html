📖 Our Story

Preserving the Kopitiam Spirit

In 1973, Uncle Lim opened his first stall at Tiong Bahru Market with a simple promise: to brew the perfect cup of kopi for his neighbors. Using nothing but a trusty coffee sock, a charcoal stove, and beans roasted in the backyard, he created a morning ritual that would span generations.

"A kopitiam isn't just a shop. It's the living room of the neighborhood, where everyone is welcome."
— Uncle Lim, Founder
