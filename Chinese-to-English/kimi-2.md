### Key Design Decisions

- **Readability comes last** in the preservation hierarchy — meaning, technical correctness, logical structure, rhetorical force, and formatting all take precedence over elegant prose.
- **"Confirmed"** is the sole canonical translation for "成立" in verification contexts — no alternation with "Valid," "True," or "Verified."
- **Product names are sacred** — CubeSandbox, RustVMM, KVM, PVM, XFS, FICLONE, etc. never translate.
- **Numbers are immutable** — latency, memory, version numbers, dates, percentages, ranges all preserved exactly.
- **The 7-phase pipeline is non-negotiable** — each phase catches a distinct category of error that no other phase addresses.

This skill is designed to function as a reusable operational manual that any capable LLM can follow to produce translations approaching professional human technical translator quality.
